Project Overview

This roguelike multiplayer game is a passion project that combines classic roguelike mechanics with multiplayer gameplay. Players navigate procedurally generated dungeons, collect items, level up their characters, and develop unique skill builds to overcome increasingly difficult challenges.

The project demonstrates my ability to design complex game systems, implement networking for multiplayer functionality, and create engaging gameplay loops.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Leveling System: Players gain experience and level up, unlocking new abilities and stat increases that allow for diverse character builds
  • Interactive Items: A wide variety of items with unique effects that can synergize in unexpected ways, creating emergent gameplay moments
  • Skill Trees: Multiple skill paths that allow players to specialize their character and adapt to different playstyles
  • Multiplayer Networking: Seamless cooperative gameplay where players can team up to tackle bosses together
  • Procedural Generation: Each playthrough offers a unique experience with randomly generated levels and loot
  • Roguelike Mechanics: Permadeath and run-based progression that encourages replayability and mastery

Technical Challenges

Multiplayer Synchronization: Implementing robust networking to ensure all players see consistent game state, especially with fast-paced combat and real-time item interactions.

Balancing Systems: Designing a leveling and item system that feels rewarding without becoming overpowered, requiring extensive playtesting and iteration.

Procedural Generation: Creating algorithms that generate interesting, playable levels while maintaining performance and ensuring fair difficulty curves.

What I Learned

This project has been an invaluable learning experience in game development. I've gained deep expertise in GDScript and the Godot Engine, learned to implement complex multiplayer systems, and developed strong skills in game design and balancing.

Beyond the technical skills, I've learned the importance of iterative design, player feedback, and the art of creating compelling game loops that keep players engaged. Managing a long-term solo project has also taught me discipline, time management, and the persistence needed to bring ambitious ideas to life.

Future Development

  • More enemy types and boss encounters
  • Enhanced multiplayer features

  • Improved UI/UX for inventory and skill management
